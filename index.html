About Section

Boulevard is a Michelin Starred restaurant located in the heart of Dublin City Centre. Our food is locally sourced. The natural course of the Seasons dictates our ever-changing and evolving menu. The kitchen is led by chef patron Leah Soanes. Alongside using wild and foraged local ingredients, Leah likes to foster old traditional cooking and preservation methods, such as curing, pickling, smoking and fermenting. Her aim is to look back in order to look forward: to the future of Irish food. It is only by understanding our culinary past that we can unlock the untapped resources of our future. Get ready to experience Irish food through the ages with a contemporary filter. Our tasting menu is an immersive dining experience of Irish food through the ages with a contemporary filter.
